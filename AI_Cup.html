競賽介紹 Competition Overview

面對企業ESG報告中普遍存在的「漂綠」問題，運用AI技術開發永續承諾驗證系統，提升企業永續資訊揭露的透明度與可信度。

Addressing the widespread "greenwashing" issues in corporate ESG reports, we use AI technology to develop sustainability promise verification systems, enhancing the transparency and credibility of corporate sustainability information disclosure.

承諾語句識別

Promise Identification

判斷給定的句子是否表達企業對未來行動的明確承諾，辨識真實承諾與空泛宣言。

Determine whether a given sentence expresses a clear commitment to future actions by the company.

支持性證據連結

Evidence Linking

評估已識別的承諾語句是否附有具體執行計畫或支持佐證。

Evaluate whether identified promises are accompanied by specific implementation plans or supporting evidence.

清晰度判斷

Clarity Assessment

分析承諾語句是否語意明確、無模糊字眼，識別潛在的「漂綠」風險。

Analyze whether promise statements are semantically clear without ambiguous terms.

時機推論

Timeline Inference

根據語句推論承諾的預期完成時間，建立時間驗證機制。

Infer the expected completion time of commitments based on statements.

Using Krippendorff's Alpha for consistency assessment, ensuring quality above 0.8
